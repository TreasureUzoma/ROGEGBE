The Leadership

Explaining the Leadership of Realm Of Glory Egbe

Realm Of Glory Int'l Ikotun Egbe Church has been a pillar of the community for over 10 years, providing spiritual guidance and support to the people of Ikotun-Egbe. The church is led by a dedicated team of individuals who are passionate about serving God and serving others. These leaders work tirelessly to ensure that Realm Of Glory Egbe Church remains a welcoming and nurturing place for all who enter its doors.

The associate pastor at Realm of Glory Egbe Church is a trusted and valued member of the church's leadership team. He works closely with the senior pastor to ensure that all aspects of the church's ministry are running smoothly. He is a gifted teacher and a compassionate listener who is always available to offer guidance and support to those who need it. He is an integral part of the church's mission and vision.

The pastor's wife is not only a devoted spouse, but also a gifted minister in her own right. She is a strong leader and a passionate advocate for the church's mission. She is known for her kindness, her wisdom, and her ability to connect with people from all walks of life. Together, the pastoral couple is a powerful force for good in the community.

The staff at Realm of Glory Egbe Church is a talented and dedicated group of individuals who are committed to serving God and the community. They work together as a team to ensure that the church's ministries are running smoothly and that its members are cared for and supported. From the facilities team to the music ministry, every person on staff plays a vital role in making Realm of Glory Egbe branch Church the warm and welcoming place that it is.

In summary, Realm of Glory Egbe Branch Church is a place where people can find hope, healing, and community. It is a place where the love of God is felt and experienced by all who enter its doors. The church's leadership, staff, and members are all committed to living out the gospel and making a difference in the world around them.
