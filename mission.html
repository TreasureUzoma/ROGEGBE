Our Mission

The Mission of the Realm of Glory Int'l Ikotun - Egbe Branch

The mission of Realm Of Glory Egbe Branch Church is to spread the love of God and make disciples of Jesus Christ. We believe that every person is made in the image of God and is worthy of love and respect. We strive to create a safe and welcoming community where people can grow in their faith and serve others. We value authenticity, integrity, and compassion, and we seek to live these values out in all that we do.
