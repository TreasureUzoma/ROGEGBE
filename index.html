Ethnic and Tribal Unity

Realm Of Glory Egbe branch church is committed to ethnic and tribal reconciliation, healing and unity. Our Mission is to be a healthy church changing lives through Christ. Jesus Christ is our hope - as his kingdom is His expression of His ruled and reign in and amongst the nations. This has immediate and eternal implication for human thriving. As a church centered on Jesus and His gospel, we are compelled to ensure that all people are treated with equality, justice and inclusion.
